Our Mission

This app has been created for the independent game developer. Understanding the forces of the industry, and the need to make games in currently-popular genres, to drive their profitably, and keep them in the game.

We have provided a way to examine all of Steam's tags, with their attached Metacritic scores, to allow developers to see what is hot with the gamers, and give their development cycle a solid starting point.
